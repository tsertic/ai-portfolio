About
Who I Am

Based in Zagreb, Croatia, I am a full-stack software developer at Arctis d.o.o., where I architect and deliver software solutions for some of the world's most prominent organisations — including Saudi Aramco, the European Space Agency, and the European Court of Justice.

My primary platform is Archibus IWMS, through which I handle everything from Java backend development and SQL optimisation to React-powered frontend modernisation within legacy enterprise environments. I manage server configuration across Archibus, Tomcat, and database systems, and I take pride in seeing a feature through from initial requirements to production deployment.

Outside of enterprise work, I design and build Next.js and TypeScript websites for clients — handling design, development, server deployment, and ongoing maintenance end-to-end.

What motivates me is the challenge itself: whether untangling a decade-old codebase or crafting something entirely from scratch, I find satisfaction in solutions that are both technically sound and quietly elegant.

Away from the screen: father to Anja, companion to a mixed-breed dog named Eevee, and enthusiast of basketball, cycling, chess, and the occasional dive.
