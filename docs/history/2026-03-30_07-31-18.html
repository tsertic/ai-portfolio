About me

Where code meets craft

There's something quietly satisfying about a system that just works. I've been chasing that feeling for years — first as a developer at Arctis d.o.o. in Zagreb, where I build enterprise software on the Archibus IWMS platform for some pretty serious clients: Saudi Aramco, the European Space Agency, the European Court of Justice, and the University of Luxembourg, among others.

My days are spent deep in Java backends, SQL optimizations, and the delicate art of weaving React into legacy codebases without breaking anything that's been running for a decade. It's equal parts archaeology and architecture.

After hours, I build websites for clients and friends using Next.js and TypeScript — from the first sketch in Figma all the way to the server humming in a datacenter. I also tinker with Python scripts and AI experiments, mostly for the joy of it.

When I'm not at the keyboard, you'll find me hiking the hills outside Zagreb, diving the clear Adriatic, or playing chess while Eevee — my dog — pretends to understand the game.
