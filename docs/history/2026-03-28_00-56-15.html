The Thinking Behind the Code

Here's the paradox of enterprise software: the more critical the system, the less glamorous the work. I spend my professional days at Arctis d.o.o. building and extending Archibus IWMS — the platform that keeps facilities running for organizations like Saudi Aramco, the European Space Agency, and the European Court of Justice. Java backends, SQL surgery, React transplants into legacy codebases, server orchestration — the full lifecycle from requirements through deployment and beyond.

When the enterprise hat comes off, I build Next.js + TypeScript websites for clients and friends — from concept and design through deployment and ongoing care. What genuinely drives me is the puzzle-solving: whether it's resurrecting a decade-old system or architecting something from a blank terminal. My daughter Anja and our dog Eevee ensure I occasionally step away from the screen — but only occasionally.
