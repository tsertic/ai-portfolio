About

The Craft & The Code

Based in Zagreb, Croatia, I have spent the better part of my career at Arctis d.o.o., where the work is anything but routine. The platform is Archibus — an enterprise IWMS used by some of the world's most demanding organizations — and my role spans the full spectrum: Java backend modules, SQL optimization, front-end interfaces, React integration into decade-old codebases, and hands-on server configuration for Archibus, Tomcat, and the databases beneath them.

The client list speaks to the scale of the work: Saudi Aramco, the European Space Agency, the European Court of Justice, the University of Luxembourg, and others. Each deployment brings its own constraints and expectations. I handle everything from initial requirements through to deployment and long-term maintenance.

Outside the enterprise world, I design and build Next.js + TypeScript websites for clients — taking projects from the first wireframe through to live server deployment. What drives me is the same in both contexts: solving a hard problem cleanly, and leaving the system better than I found it.
