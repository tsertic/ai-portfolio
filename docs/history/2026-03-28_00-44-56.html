The Human Behind the IDE

Let's cut to it: I'm a full-stack developer at Arctis d.o.o. in Zagreb, doing the kind of work that sounds way cooler at parties than it actually is — extending Archibus IWMS, an enterprise workplace management platform, for clients like Saudi Aramco, the European Space Agency, and the European Court of Justice.


Yes, ESA. Yes, I'm basically helping manage the offices of the people who launch rockets. No, I don't get to touch the rockets.


The work involves Java backend modules, SQL optimization that would make a DBA cry (in a good way), dragging legacy systems into the React era, and server configuration that absolutely works on the first try. Sometimes.


When the day job is done, I build Next.js + TypeScript websites — from design to deployment — because apparently I like computers even on my days off. I also have a daughter named Anja and a dog named Eevee, both of whom have better social skills than most enterprise software.
