The Blueprint

Based in the coastal resonance of Zagreb, Croatia, I spend my cycles orchestrating high-stakes software solutions for the likes of Saudi Aramco and the European Space Agency via the Archibus IWMS platform.


I specialize in the delicate art of bridging legacy enterprise backends with the reactive fluidity of modern frontends—optimizing SQL queries by day and exploring the frontiers of Next.js and AI by night. Whether it's setting up Tomcat servers or fine-tuning React components, I build for scale, stability, and intellectual rigor.


Off the clock, I'm likely hiking the local trails with Eevee (my four-legged co-pilot) or teaching my daughter Anja the logic of the world.
