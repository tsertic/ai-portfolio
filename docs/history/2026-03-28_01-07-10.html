The Story So Far

A maker of things, a solver of riddles the machines leave behind.

There is a quiet drama in coaxing life from systems that were never meant to bend. That is the work I do — day after day, from Zagreb, for the world. At Arctis d.o.o., I build and extend the Archibus platform for names that carry weight: Saudi Aramco, the European Space Agency, the European Court of Justice, the University of Luxembourg. Java on the back end, SQL that sings, and front-end layers stitched with HTML, JavaScript, and CSS.

But the real thrill? Weaving React into legacy architectures, proving that old and new can share the same breath. Setting up servers, configuring Tomcat, writing documentation that someone will actually read. The full arc — from first requirement to final deployment.

Outside those walls, I build with Next.js and TypeScript for clients who need the web done right. From design to server, from pixel to production. When the screens go dark, you'll find me with Anja, my daughter, and Eevee, the dog who insists on supervising everything.
