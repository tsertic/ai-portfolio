Who am I? Good question.

I’m a developer based in Zagreb, Croatia. By day, I’m deep in the trenches of legacy enterprise systems at Arctis d.o.o., building solutions for massive names like Saudi Aramco and the European Space Agency. By night, I’m usually wrestling with React or trying to explain to my dog, Eevee, why the API isn't responding.

I live for solving technical problems that make most people want to throw their monitor out a window. Whether it's optimizing SQL queries that have been slow since 2008 or bridging React into ancient IWMS platforms, I’m your guy. Also, my daughter Anja thinks I just "push buttons," which is technically correct.
