The Dossier

Who's behind the code.

I'm a full-stack developer working out of Zagreb, Croatia. By day, I build and maintain enterprise software at Arctis d.o.o., where we develop solutions on the Archibus IWMS platform for organizations like Saudi Aramco, the European Space Agency, the European Court of Justice, and the University of Luxembourg.

My professional work spans the full lifecycle — Java backend modules, SQL optimization, HTML/JS/CSS frontends, React integrations into legacy systems, server configuration (Tomcat, Archibus, databases), and technical documentation. I handle everything from gathering requirements to deployment and long-term maintenance.

Outside of work, I build Next.js and TypeScript websites for clients and friends, tinker with AI experiments, write utility scripts, and generally chase down whatever technical problem catches my attention. I'm a father to Anja and the reluctant owner of a mixed-breed dog named Eevee, who has yet to write a single line of code.
