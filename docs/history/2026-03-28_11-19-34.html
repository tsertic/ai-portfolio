The Maker

A little about who I am

By day, Tomislav shapes the invisible architecture of global enterprise — bending Archibus into something that actually works for the European Space Agency, Saudi Aramco, and the halls of the European Court of Justice. It is unglamorous work, sometimes. It is also deeply satisfying.

He lives in Zagreb, Croatia, writes Java and JavaScript with equal calm, and genuinely enjoys the puzzle of making a fifteen-year-old system behave like it was built this morning. React, injected into legacy code like a quiet revolution — that sort of thing.

After hours, the work becomes smaller and warmer: Next.js sites for friends and clients, Python scripts that do strange useful things, AI experiments that occasionally surprise even him.

At home: a daughter named Anja, a dog named Eevee, and the occasional chess match lost on purpose.
