The Blueprint

I'm a Zagreb-based Full-Stack Developer navigating the complex intersections of legacy enterprise infrastructure and cutting-edge web technologies. My daily rhythm involves orchestrating the Archibus IWMS platform for high-stakes clients including Saudi Aramco, the European Space Agency, and the University of Luxembourg.


I specialize in bridging the gap between SQL-heavy backends and fluid React-based interfaces. Whether it's optimizing server configurations on Tomcat or crafting technical documentation for sprawling modular systems, I thrive on solving the puzzles others find too daunting.


Beyond the office, you'll find me building bespoke Next.js ecosystems for clients, hanging out with my daughter Anja, or exploring the trails with Eevee, my mixed-breed companion.
