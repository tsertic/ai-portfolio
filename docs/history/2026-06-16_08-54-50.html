The "Professional" Bio

I’m a full-stack developer based in Zagreb. I spend my working hours at Arctis d.o.o., where we build massive IWMS solutions for clients who don't have time for bugs—think Saudi Aramco, the European Space Agency, and the European Court of Justice.

My day-to-day is a mix of optimizing SQL queries that would make a grown man cry, Java backend heavy-lifting, and trying to convince legacy Archibus systems that React is actually a good idea. It's complex, it's enterprise, and it works.

When I'm not staring at a terminal, I'm likely being managed by my daughter Anja or our dog Eevee. I also cycle and hike to remind myself that the outdoors exists.
